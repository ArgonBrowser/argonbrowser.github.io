Purp is a new name for Argon.
Why I gave a new name and what it means, look under the cut.

Why changed the name?
In Google Search, I found another browser called Argon. This is the Augmented Reality browser. I had to come up with a new unique name.

Why Purp?
I've been thinking up a new name for a long time. And I came up with the word "Purp".
The word "Purp" is an abbreviation for the word "purple".
